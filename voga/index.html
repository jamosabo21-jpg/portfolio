Design process

The objective of the interface design was to replicate the presented proposal's appearance for the store's homepage. This aimed at establishing a consistent and unified look, promoting cohesion with the overall design elements.


After establishing a consistent design system for the website, the next goal was to extend and apply it to the design of an iOS app.

04 App

Mobile App Design

Benchmark

In the benchmark exercise, I pinpointed strengths and weaknesses in each reference app, identifying commonalities among them. Armed with this insight, I considered the mental models presented by these apps to craft a familiar and user-friendly design for the Vogas app.

User task flow: Place order

Wireframes

I utilized the proposed design for the new website to create wireframes, employing components closely aligned with the proposal. After completing the main flow, the wireframes underwent refinement to achieve the final proposed result for the app.

05 APP

The Vogas app design prioritizes offering users an easy and intuitive experience for purchasing furniture directly through the app. Users have the opportunity to access the store from anywhere, with a key focus on creating a seamless and user-friendly shopping experience
