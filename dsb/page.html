Stewardship principles


Embrace equity, diversity and accessibility in your project, and respect local communities and cultures.

Product lifecycle



Prioritize the maintainability, longevity, and reusability of your project throughout its lifespan to promote sustainability.

Clean



Minimize digital waste and carbon footprint across your technology stack by prioritizing the use of renewable energy and sustainable cloud practices.

Avoiding manipulative design

Promotes transparency, user respect, and ethical design principles, avoiding manipulative design patterns that may negatively impact users.

Operational digital efficiency

Optimize performance and resource usage to improve user experience by committing to digital sustainability practices.
