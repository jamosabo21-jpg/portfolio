Brand fundaments

How Joinco
Became Joinco

Goal

The goal here was to create an identity that truly captures the spirit of Joinco, a creative brand offering UX consulting to a wide range of clients.
At its core, Joinco is all about building deep, genuine connections. The brand needed to reflect that, prioritizing human relationships.

The idea was to design a space where every client, every project, and every interaction feels like it belongs, contributing to something bigger, meaningful, and cohesive.

Solution

A clear, recognizable identity was key to capturing Joincos spirit. Its flexible, collaborative nature comes through in the smooth, continuous lines of the typography and icons.
The dynamic icon, just a line and a dot, distills the brand into its simplest, most memorable form.
